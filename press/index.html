The games, in one line each

  • Doppit. A daily logic puzzle. You drag dominoes onto a constraint-region grid. doppitgame.com
  • Town Detective. A geo-guess game. You see a city from above and try to name it. towndetective.com
  • Historical Events (built with Joey). A history-trivia game with four modes, multiplayer, 66 achievements, and six languages. historical.towndetective.com
  • Pet Cafe. A cozy tycoon. The cute SVG sprites are real, but so is the economic simulation underneath. petcafegame.com
  • State of Fortune. A 2D space opera honoring the Escape Velocity series. stateoffortune.com

About the studio

A small indie studio.

MiLu is a small indie studio. I'm Mike, the founder.

What I make: a logic puzzle, a geography game, a cozy tycoon, a history-trivia game I built with my son Joey, and a 2D space opera that's been in progress for a while. Five games live, more coming.

Every game runs in your browser. No accounts, no installs. Free for now, and when that has to change I'll be honest about why.
